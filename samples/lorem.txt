Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
What is Lorem Ipsum?
It’s a block of made-up text—mostly Latin words—that designers and developers drop into layouts to hold space until real copy is ready.
Where does Lorem Ipsum come from?
The words were lifted from a 45 BC philosophical work by the Roman writer Cicero and were chopped up by early printers in the 1500s to test type.
What does “Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet” mean?
In the original Latin, the phrase referred to “pain itself,” but because later printers scrambled the sentence order, the line no longer carries a clear meaning.
Why do designers use Lorem Ipsum?
Its word and line lengths resemble normal prose, so you can judge fonts, spacing, and overall layout without being distracted by readable content.
Is Lorem Ipsum real Latin?
Yes, the individual words are Latin, but the grammar has been jumbled so the passage doesn’t read like proper Latin or convey a sensible message.
Is Lorem Ipsum copyrighted?
No. Cicero’s original text is in the public domain, and the rearranged filler version is free for anyone to use.
Can I translate Lorem Ipsum into English?
You can translate single words with a Latin dictionary, but full sentences won’t come out right because the text was rearranged centuries ago.
How do I insert Lorem Ipsum in Microsoft Word?
Type =lorem(p,s) in a blank paragraph, then press Enter. Replace p with the number of paragraphs and s with sentences per paragraph.
How do I add Lorem Ipsum to an HTML page?
Use any online generator to create the text, then copy and paste it inside your HTML tags—either as plain paragraphs or with headings, lists, and quotes.
Why is Lorem Ipsum the default filler in so many themes and templates?
Because printing kits, desktop-publishing tools, and website builders have bundled it for decades, it’s become the universal stand-in whenever a layout needs sample text fast.
